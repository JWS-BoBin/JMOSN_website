Seed&Spark Campaign Launches to Fund Completion of Heartfelt Film "Juliette’s Miracle on St. Nick"

New York, NY, September 5th, 2024 — The team behind the upcoming independent film Juliette’s Miracle on St. Nick is excited to announce the launch of their Seed&Spark campaign on Friday, September 6th, at noon. The fundraising initiative will support the film’s final stages of production, helping to bring this deeply personal and moving story to audiences everywhere.

Juliette’s Miracle on St. Nick follows the journey of Juliette, an elderly woman grappling with her mortality in hospice. As she reflects on her life, Juliette receives a visit from an unlikely visitor—Mr. Cat, a mystical feline who offers her the chance to relive one magical night from her youth.

Through this surreal and enchanting experience, Juliette is transported back to the vibrant jazz era of Harlem, where she rediscovers her love of dance and reconnects with cherished memories of her past. Juliette navigates a bustling club and revisits her beloved garden, where she encounters Mudear, a wise elder who helps her reflect on life, family, and the importance of the moments that shape us.

Inspired by the personal experiences of writer and director Antwand Pearman, Juliette’s Miracle on St. Nick is based on his grandmother’s life. Currently residing in a nursing home, Pearman’s grandmother serves as the emotional core of this poignant tale about life, love, and the legacies we leave behind.

“This film is a love letter to my grandmother and the powerful connections we share with those we hold dear, even in life’s final moments,” said Pearman. “Through Juliette’s story, we want to explore the universal themes of family, memory, and the moments that stay with us forever.”

With the Seed&Spark campaign, the team hopes to raise the necessary funds to complete the project. Supporters of the campaign will have the opportunity to join the film’s journey and earn unique rewards such as exclusive behind-the-scenes content, digital downloads, and even a special thank-you in the film’s credits.

About the Seed&Spark Campaign

Launching on Friday at noon, the Seed&Spark campaign for Juliette’s Miracle on St. Nick will help the team push the film toward completion.
