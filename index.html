About the Film

In "Juliette's Miracle on St. Nick," we embark on a journey with Juliette, an elderly woman facing the twilight of her life in a Harlem hospital. Struggling with the inevitability of her mortality, Juliette receives an unexpected visit from Mr. Cat, a mystical feline figure, who offers her a chance to relive one enchanted night of her youth.

Transported back to the vibrant jazz era of Harlem, Juliette rediscovers the joy of dance and reconnects with her cherished memories. With Mr. Cat as her guide, she navigates through the bustling streets and finds herself at her beloved garden, where she encounters Mudear, a wise elder who shares in Juliette's reflections on life and family.
